Important Disclaimer

ETA Assist provides application processing services only. We are not directly affiliated with any government agency. We submit and process your eTA application on your behalf and then await the issuing authority's decision to approve or deny the application. All final decisions are made by the relevant government authority.

Refund Policy

In the event an application is denied by the government authority, ETA Assist will provide a 65% refund of the service fee paid to us. This refund applies to our service charge only and does not include any non-refundable government fees, third-party charges, or transaction fees.
